What is RollPlayRadio?

You've just created your first table-top adventure, the story is ready and so are the players, but what about the atmosphere? That's where we come in!

With an offering of ambience, music, action and enemy sound cards, for you to build the perfect soundscape for your session!

Who is RollPlayRad.io?

At this point it is just developed by one learning front-end developer, me! YoghurtJones!

Slowly teaching myself the front-end fundamentals of web development.

After hearing from friends who play and lead table-top fantasy games such as Dungeons and Dragons, always discovering the same over-complicated challenge of audio from existing offerings on the internet.

The objective is to provide a simple UI to deliver audio tracks that can be independently controlled across standard audio options.

The audio found on this website are composed by creative contacts of mine and/or licensed from talented artists. We have a credits page for every track, just incase you would like to find more by the composer.
